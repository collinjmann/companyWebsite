...Always Progressing
Vendicant Games

We value people. We value culture. We value the family. When we are inclusive, we embrace the whole person and enable individual participation that promotes the value of the individual.

By embracing quality standards, processes, and techniques, we demonstrate continuous improvement of our products for the benefit our consumers. Nothing less than 100% is satisfactory.

We want to take care of our portion of our space on this planet. By embracing green initiatives, we inspire each other to seek new ways to recycle and be better consumers of our resources.

Our Mission

At Vendicant Games, we seek to create innovative, casual games that compete in the indie development world. Through the use of digital marketing, we strive to establish and build a strong and unique brand. In order to be responsible citizens both corporate, locally and regionally, we place high value on people development and recycling.
